How does opt-in/out work? GDPR? Israeli Privacy Law?
↓
"The question isn't can they opt out — it's can they see exactly what flows. Transparency replaces the opt-out dilemma. When employees have a live receipt, they don't need to opt out. They need to trust what they can verify."
GDPR
On-device processing + k-anonymity (k≥5) + local differential privacy before any data leaves — compliant by architecture
Israeli Privacy Law
Org is the data controller, TAB is the processor — standard DPA covers this. Employee sees their own data always.
Pause button
Any employee can pause TAB entirely at any time — one tap. No explanation required. No flag to manager.
Design principle
If an employee can't opt out without fear — it's surveillance. Our trust ladder makes opting in the obvious choice.

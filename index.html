TAB@Work
Before we start —
here's exactly what TAB sees.
TAB is your execution layer — not a monitor, not a judge. Before it does anything, you decide what it can observe. This is a real choice, not a formality.
Morning briefing required
TAB reads your calendar and open tasks to prepare your day. No content is stored — only structure.
Friction detection
TAB notices when you've been stuck — long doc, idle time, tab switching. Patterns only, no content.
Anonymous org contribution
Aggregated patterns (never you individually) flow to your org's infrastructure dashboard. Minimum k=5 anonymity.
Intervention feedback
When you rate a bridge (helpful / not), that signal trains TAB for your patterns. On-device only.
You can pause TAB entirely at any time — one tap, no explanation needed, nothing flagged to your manager.

What TAB learns from each — honestly
From Microsoft Viva
Meeting load signals are real and measurable
They proved meeting density correlates with burnout. TAB uses this signal — anonymously, not individually.
From Cluely
Real-time intervention UX works — when done right
Cluely proved people accept in-session AI prompts. TAB uses the same pattern transparently, ethically, without the covert angle.
From Salesforce Einstein
Predictive next-best-action has traction
The "what should I do next" prompt is proven in sales. TAB extends it to every role, not just revenue teams.
From Notion AI
Knowledge linking reduces cognitive load
Connecting related context without asking the user to search is exactly what TAB's morning briefing does for tasks and workflows.
From Google Workspace
Calendar context is the best daily anchor
Google proved calendar-aware AI feels natural. TAB uses this as the foundation for the morning brief — then goes further.
From Slack AI
Async summary cuts communication overhead
Thread summarization reduces re-reading tax. TAB absorbs this into overnight automation — surfaces only what needs the human.
